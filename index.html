About us

Leaf & Soul was born out of a simple desire—to restore tea to its true essence. We are tired of the fast pace of life, where even a cup of tea has become just a drink “on the go.”

Our founder traveled extensively throughout Asia, seeking inspiration in the traditional tea ceremonies of Japan, China, and India. He saw how tea is treated there not as a product, but as a ritual — a moment to pause, breathe, and find peace.

This is how the Leaf & Soul brand came into being—tea that combines the leaves of nature and the soul of man. We select only hand-picked leaves from organic plantations, dried in the sun, without flavorings or colorings.

Each of our blends is created with one idea in mind: that every sip should bring peace, inspiration, and balance.
